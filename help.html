1. Getting Started

Creating an Account

Follow these steps to create your account:

  • Visit the MoneyMinder homepage.
  • Click on the "Sign Up" button at the top-right corner.
  • Enter your email address and create a password.
  • Confirm your email and log in.

2. Tracking Your Expenses

Adding Expenses

To add a new expense, follow these steps:

  • Click on the "My Expense" button.
  • Enter the expense Amount, Currency, Category, Date and Additional info.
  • Click "Add Expense" to record the expense.

3. Budgeting

Creating a Budget

To create a budget:

  • Navigate to the "My Budget" section.
  • Click "Create New Budget" and set limits for each category.
  • Click "Save" to activate your budget.

4. Reports & Insights

Viewing Expense Reports

To view detailed reports:

  • Go to the "My monthly expenditure" section.
  • Select the type of report (e.g., Monthly or Category Breakdown).
  • Click "Generate Report" to view it.
